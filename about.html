The Groove Garden

Welcome to Groove Garden, a dynamic and energetic band that blends funk, rock, and soul into a unique and infectious sound. Our music is all about creating a fun and vibrant atmosphere that gets people dancing and feeling good. Whether you're a fan of classic funk or modern pop, there's something for everyone in the Groove Garden.

ABOUT

Our Groovy Journey

Groove Garden was formed in 2015 by four friends who shared a passion for music and a desire to create something fresh and exciting. Over the years, we've honed our sound and built a loyal fanbase, playing at clubs, festivals, and special events all over the country. Our music has been described as a modern take on classic funk, with a mix of driving basslines, funky guitar riffs, and soulful vocals that get people moving
